SAFETY

The driving technology of the GV70 removes even the smallest risk factors between drivers, cars, and pedestrians, leaving only two essential elements: safety and convenience.
PERFORMANCE

The driving performance features of the GV70 enable consistently stable and accurate driving even under constantly changing driving and road conditions.

CONVENIENCE FEATURE

When the rear door or tailgate is opened, the driver is alerted with a warning sound and a cluster pop-up when the driver disembarks. If the driver fails to notice the alarm and locks the car door, the radar sensor mounted on the rear detects movement and provides a second notification via horn, emergency lights, and SMS.
